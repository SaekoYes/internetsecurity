The Basics of Maintaining Security on The Internet

Never give out your personal information to people you don't know.

This includes your name, address, phone number, and password.

Be careful what websites you visit.

Some websites can be phishing scams which pose as a legitimate site login pages in order to steal your information.

Use a strong password only you know.

Your password should contain uppercase letters and special characters. DO NOT SHARE YOUR PASSWORD WITH ANYONE.

If something seems sketchy make sure to block and report it

Platforms tend to have features that allow users to report content they deem unsafe.
