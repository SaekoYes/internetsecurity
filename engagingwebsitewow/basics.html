The Basics of Maintaining Security on The Internet

Never give out your personal information to people you don't know.

This includes your name, address, phone number, and password.

Be careful what websites you visit.

Some websites can be phishing scams which pretend to be a site's login page to steal your information.

Use a strong password only you know.

Your password should contain uppercase letters and special characters, for example, ! @ # %. DO NOT SHARE YOUR PASSWORD WITH ANYONE.

If you see something that looks suspicious, block it and tell someone about it.

Platforms have features that allow you to report things you think is unsafe.
