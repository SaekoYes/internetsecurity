Additional Tips for Maintaining Security on The Internet

Keep your software up to date

Software updates often include security fixes that can help protect you from bad people trying to steal your information.

Use an ad blocker on your browser

ads contain trackers that intrude on your right to privacy in order to sell the "right" products to you and make more money. An adblock can prevent this.

Be careful about what you download from the internet

Only download from trusted websites. Some websites could be offering different downloads that might contain viruses.
