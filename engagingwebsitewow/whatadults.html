For Adults

Be aware of the risks and benefits of giving a child access to the internet.

The internet can be a great resource for children but with those benefits come dangers, including but not limited to cyberbullying and inappropriate content.

Respect the privacy of your children on your own socials.

Do not share pictures of your children on social media. Even if it is a private account the wrong people can still access them through the right means.

Set boundaries for internet and screen time usage using parental controls.

Use parental controls to set limits on screen time and block inappropriate content. This helps children enjoy the pleasures of the internet while also maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

In a non-invasive way monitor young people's activity on the internet.

Non-invasive ways to monitor young people's internet activity include talking to them about the monitoring instead of forcing it without reason.
